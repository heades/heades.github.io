--- layout: subpage title: A Visit from SPAWAR's Dr. Evan Austin sitemap: priority: 0.9 changefreq: monthly --- The United States Naval Warfare Centers, Systems Centers, and Naval Research Laboratory are collectively known as the Naval Research and Development Establishment (NR&DE). Dr. Evan Austin, is a research scientist at one of the NR&DE's member laboratories, Space and Naval Warfare (SPAWAR) Systems Center Atlantic in Charleston, South Carolina. He is visiting the School of Computer and Cyber Sciences (SCCS) to discuss any type of research collaborations, or other types of partnerships, between SPAWAR and Augusta University. He will be presenting to the students and faculty of SCCS, but everyone is welcome to attend.
