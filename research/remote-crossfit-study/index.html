How are CrossFit Athletes Remotely Coached?

The purpose of this study is to develop an understanding of the current practices and technology used by remotely coached athletes and their coaches. This study consists of 2 important parts:

  • Survey: provide a view of the technology used for remote coaching, the number of coaches offering remote coaching, and the number of remotely coached athletes.
  • Interviews: understand the coaches/athletes overall satisfaction with the technology and processes they use to provide/obtain remote coaching.

Eligibility
  1. Must be 18 years old or older.
  2. At least one of the following must be true:
    1. Be a CrossFit coach, functional fitness coach, strength coach or olympic weightlifting coach who offers remote coaching.
    2. Be a remotely-coached CrossFit athlete, functional fitness athlete, strength athlete, or olympic weightlifting athlete who is coached remotely.

Potential Benefits

This study may lead to a better understanding of how to design remote-coaching technology to be easier to use, more efficient, more accessible, and more enjoyable for both athletes and coaches.
